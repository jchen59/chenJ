    Setup

    Starting off, this should be written in the Head of your HTML file.

    <link rel=stylesheet type=text/css href="https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/foundation/6.5.0/css/foundation.css">
    This will let you use all of Foundation's stylings.
